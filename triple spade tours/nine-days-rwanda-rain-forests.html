9 Days Rwanda’s Rain forests(NYUNGWE National Park), Apes & Primates, Lake Kivu, Gorilla and Akagera park Naional Park.

Track both of Rwanda’s great apes – mountain gorillas and chimpanzees – and see some of the country’s other specialised and striking primates and Nyungwe Canopy walk and Tea plantation experience as well. The cost of this trip is 21,395$ per person.

Start

Arrive in Kigali, meet by your guide, spend the night at a hand-picked hotel.

Day 1

Visit the Kigali Genocide Memorial, drive south to Butare.

Day 2

Enjoy a chimpanzee trek in the morning, spend the afternoon on the canopy walk away, overnight at hand-picked lodge.

Day 3

Guided Bird watching / walk , Rwenzori colobus in the morning before driving north for lunch at Kibuye and an afternoon transfer north to Gisenyi, overnight at hand-picked hotel.

Day 4

Drive to the Virunga Volcanoes, enjoy an inspiring gorilla trek, overnight at hand-picked lodge.

Day 5

Discover the other endemic primate – the golden monkey, experience local culture, overnight at hand-picked lodge

Day 6

Discover Akagera national park, and enjoy a boat ride.

Day 7

Enjoy the game driver.

Day 8

Back to Kigali for return flight.
