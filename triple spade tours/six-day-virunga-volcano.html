6 Day Virunga Volcanoes Mountain Gorilla

Spend 4 nights beneath the dramatic peaks of the Virunga Volcanoes enjoying two intimate and moving one-hour encounters with a family of mountain gorillas. The cost of this trip is $6,530 per person .

Start

Arrive in Kigali, meet by your guide, spend the night at a hand-picked hotel.

Day 1

Visit the Kigali Genocide Memorial, drive to the Virunga Volcanoes, overnight at a hand-picked lodge.

Day 2

Enjoy your first mountain gorilla trek, afternoon at leisure, and overnight at hand-picked lodge.

Day 3

Discover Diana Fossy Museum in the park and the other endemic primate, the golden monkey, afternoon at leisure, overnight at hand-picked lodge.

Day 4

Enjoy your second hiking chain of volcanoes ( Gahinga, Muhabura ,Bisoke , and Kalisimbi )experience Twin Lakes ( Burera and Ruhondo) and experience local culture dance , overnight at hand-picked lodge.

Day 5

Visit local produce and textile markets, back to Kigali for return flight.
